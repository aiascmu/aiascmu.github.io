Membership Cards

Description:

Membership cards are introduced this year to give YOU even more reason to be a member. The purpose of these cards is to provide small rewards and incentives for attending events. Though we keep attendance at all events, it is your responsibility keep your card safe and to get it stamped by a board member. We are not responsible for any lost cards, so keep it safe and keep your name on it.

The following DO NOT count towards membership card stamps:

Coffee o'Clock events
Mindfulness Day events
Any fundraiser open to the public

Rewards:

5 stamps: Free Coffee or Donut at a Coffee o'Clock event
10 stamps: Free AIAS Swag
The top 15% of members with the highest number of stamps will recieve an invitation to an exclusive event at the end of the academic year. Details TBA!!!

FAQ:

Q. What if I go to so many events I fill all 10 stamp spots?
A. You can fill as many cards as you want, so if you complete an entire card, talk to a board member and we'll get you another card so you can start going to more events.
Q. What if I lose my card??
A. Sorry! We aren't responsible for any lost or stolen cards, so please keep it safe and write your name on it.
