AIA Pittsburgh: Young Architects Forum (YAF)
About YAF from YAF themselves: The AIA Pittsburghâ€™s Young Architects Forum (YAF) seeks to support and encourage young professionals in the field of architecture to obtain their license, become more involved with career development opportunities, and engage allied professionals through collaboration. We continuously seek out new and fresh ideas and the energy and resources to implement them, and have fun doing it.
Our chapter is always looking for ways to bring in professionals and young architects to network, talk about their experiences, and work with us. Our AIA-YAF Liaison attends meetings with YAF every week and creates events in conjunction with them to provide more professional programming for our members. Events include our annual CRIT night, after-lecture mixers, YAF Storeytelling, and Networking with YAF.
